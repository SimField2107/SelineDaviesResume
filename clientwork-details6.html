PepsoCo & Pop Works

Bridget Jones on pack instant win

  • Client brief: Develop the front-end for a multi-region prize claim site supporting a PepsiCo x Bridget Jones promotional campaign. Winners of cinema tickets, Uber Eats vouchers, and branded merchandise are sent unique claim links to submit deleivery information.
  • Project date: January 2025
  • Project URL: https://www.popworksbridgetjoneswinners.co.uk/

Built with: Figma · HTML5 · SCSS · Javascript· Twig · PHP

- Wireframe implementation based on client-provided structure, tailored for mobile-first experience.
- Responsive header and footer build, aligning with PepsiCo brand guidelines and legal footer requirements.
- Custom claim gate page: "You need a unique link" screen to restrict access.
- Error-state UX: "Link expired / already used" page with contextual messaging.
- Prize claim forms:
- Main & wrap-up prize forms with dynamic country selector and conditional address fields for UK and ROI (Eircode logic, optional fields).
- Voucher claim form with simplified fields for fast submission.
- Thank you pages confirming successful claims and reinforcing prize info.
- T&Cs & Privacy pages implemented with clean layout and accessible markup.
- State-based pages: “Promotion not started” / “Promotion ended” with route gating.
- Fully coded email templates for automated voucher fulfilment, with dynamic copy based on prize type (cinema vs Deliveroo).
- Device-agnostic layout and cross-browser testing for consistent performance.
- Cookie notice and Google Tag Manager integration for analytics and compliance.
- Adjusted all front-end logic and asset loading for deployment within PepsiCo’s secure hosting environment.
