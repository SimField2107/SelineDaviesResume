Spin To win

  • Client brief: AR-powered campaign Consumers, users scanned a QR code on bottle to launch an augmented reality prize wheel in their surroundings, offering them the chance to “Spin to Win.” Winners were then faced with a bold “Stick or Twist” mechanic, giving them the option to keep their prize or gamble for something better.
  • Project date: July 2024
  • Project URL:

Built with: JavaScript · Adobe PhotoShop · HTML5 · CSS Sprites

- Developed an AR-powered “Spin to Win” microsite accessible via QR codes on 1.68 million bottles.
- Integrated immersive camera-tracked prize wheel experience that launched in consumers’ environments.
- Built a “Stick or Twist” mechanic that encouraged strategic user participation.
- Implemented email verification, IP recognition and CAPTCHA to reduce fraudulent entries.
- Integrated AI chatbot for customer service, resulting in a 63% drop in consumer queries.
- Delivered a fully responsive experience optimized for high traffic and retailer-specific POS.

Highlights: Running July to December 2024, the campaign delivered exceptional results: 293,000+ entries, 109,000+ marketing opt-ins, a 17.5% redemption rate, and a 14.8% uplift in sales—all while reducing customer service queries by over 60%.
